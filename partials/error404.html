An endless void

Brashly, you close your eyes and leap through a door you thought was there. Upon opening your eyes again, however, you find... Nothing. Just a moment ago, you were so certain you would find a room at this location, but all your bravado, everything you thought you knew about the world, vanished in an instant. All around you, there is nothing but a featureless void stretching out into infinity in every direction. "What was I even trying to accomplish?" you lament as you desperately look for a way out of this madness, to no avail. Slowly but inexorably, a single intrusive thought pushes its way to the forefront of your consciousness; "Error 404: Page not found."
